The NAFAS Ecosystem
One Integrated System. Filter → Sensor → Data → Action.
NAFAS is not just a monitoring website. It is a complete ecosystem: a physical filter installed on diesel exhaust pipes that captures PM2.5, an embedded sensor that transmits live air quality data, and an AI platform that maps, predicts, and alerts.
